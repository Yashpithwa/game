How to Play - Harry Potter vs Voldemort

Welcome to the magical world of Harry Potter! In this game, you will help Harry Potter fight against the evil Voldemort.

Objective:

The goal is to defeat Voldemort by casting powerful spells and dodging his dark magic. Watch out for his attacks!

Gameplay:

  • Voldemort will appear randomly, and you must click on him to cast a spell and score points.
  • Be quick! If you wait too long or miss him, Voldemort will attack you and reduce your score.
  • The game has a time limit, and your score will be based on how many times you successfully hit Voldemort.

Winning:

The player with the highest score at the end of the time limit wins. Can you defeat Voldemort and become the hero of Hogwarts?
